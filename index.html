WEB

WEBis the common name for the World Wide Web, a subset of the Internet consisting of the pages that can be accessed by a Web browser. Many people assume that the Web is the same as the Internet, and use these terms interchangeably. However, the term Internet actually referfs to the global network of servers that makes the information sharing that happens over the Web possilbe. So, although the Web does up a large portion of the Internet, but they are not one and same.
